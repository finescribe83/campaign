Agenda

Environmental Protection

As an environmental planner, Niceforo N. Iroy brings a professional commitment to sustainable development and practical solutions for Cebu City. His expertise guides his agenda, focused on flood control, landslide prevention, and preserving natural spaces. Niceforo's proactive, expert approach offers Cebu a safer, greener, and more resilient future.

Traffic Management

As an experienced traffic consultant, Niceforo N. Iroy knows that effective traffic management requires more than just ideas—it needs strong, actionable laws. In the City Council, he will champion legislative measures to upgrade infrastructure, optimize traffic systems, and expand public transport. Community

Niceforo N. Iroy is committed to empowering Cebu’s communities by championing affordable housing, local job creation, and sustainable growth. Recognizing the potential displacement due to AI, Niceforo N. Iroy is committed to initiating a comprehensive skills inventory and targeted skill upgrades. This proactive approach will equip Cebu’s workforce with future-ready capabilities, helping them adapt and thrive in an AI-driven economy.

Niceforo N. Iroy is focused on transforming Cebu into the “Silicon Valley of the Philippines” by initiating laws and city legislation to cultivate entrepreneurial skills. His agenda includes establishing startup incubators and promoting tech innovation, providing support and resources for aspiring entrepreneurs. This vision will position Cebu as a leading hub for technology and business innovation.

About

The Milestones in Niceforo Iroy's Life as a Civil Servant and Community Leader

  • Humble Beginnings

    Born in 1961 in Albuquerque, Bohol, Niceforo Iroy grew up in a hardworking immigrant family. His parents migrated to Cebu City in search of better opportunities, where his father worked in various jobs, including as a salesman and messenger. This working-class upbringing instilled in Iroy the values of resilience, dedication, and community service.

  • Start of a Civil Service Career

    Joined the Philippine Coast Guard as an Intelligence Officer, beginning a dedicated career in public service. His role marked the start of his lifelong commitment to serving the nation and its people.

  • Expanded Service

    Moved to the Home Insurance Guarantee Corporation (HIGC), a government-owned corporation, continuing his public service career. During this time, he also completed his degree in Computer Science from the University of Visayas.

  • Higher Education & Urban Planning

    Graduated with a Diploma in Urban Planning from the University of the Philippines, Diliman. This qualification further enhanced his capability to serve communities and engage in city planning and development.

  • Masters in Public Administration

    Completed a Master’s in Public Administration at the University of the Visayas, equipping him with advanced knowledge for public governance and strengthening his resolve to serve with integrity.

  • Cebu City Planning Development

    Recruited by his classmate, Nigel Paul Villarete, to join the Cebu City Planning Development team, where he made significant contributions to the city’s growth and infrastructure.

  • Recognition for Excellence

    Received recognition from the Cebu City Government for his exemplary leadership as the Division Head of Cebu City, further solidifying his dedication to the city’s development.

  • Barangay Councilor

    Elected as Barangay Councilor of San Roque, Cebu City. His focus remains on improving local services and strengthening the community.

Spearheading the Revival of the Bangao or Simbang Gabi Tradition

Organized by Niceforo Iroy to bring the spirit of Christmas back to Cebu City.

Niceforo Iroy has led a remarkable initiative to revive Cebu City's traditional nine-day Christmas celebration, known as Bangao or Simbang Gabi. This effort unites the community in joyful preparation for Christmas, embodying Filipino hospitality, unity, and the Christmas spirit. After each dawn mass, attendees are treated to free traditional delicacies and snacks, bringing together neighbors and families in holiday cheer. Adding a unique touch, life-size camels and the Three Kings are carted to Cebu Metropolitan Cathedral to welcome churchgoers, guiding them to the area where treats are offered. This beautiful sight has captivated the community, inspiring multiple donors and fostering a modern-day spirit of bayanihan and generosity.

Consultant for Sugbuswak

An Initiative to Create Two More Diocese in Cebu

Niceforo Iroy was selected as a consultant for the Sugbuswak initiative, a project focused on dividing the Archdiocese of Cebu into smaller dioceses for better pastoral care and community outreach. His expertise in land area and population mapping will play a key role in this effort. Iroy was chosen due to his strong commitment to the Church, being an active member of Couples for Christ and a vital participant in the Family Life Apostolate. His deep involvement in church activities, coupled with his dedication to supporting families in their spiritual journey, makes him well-suited to contribute to this important initiative.

Consultant for Comprehensive Land Use Planning

Expert Guidance for Sustainable Development Across Local Governments in Cebu and Misamis Occidental

Niceforo Iroy has been tapped as a consultant for the Comprehensive Land Use Program (CLUP) in various municipalities, including Tuburan and Minglanilla in Cebu, as well as Plaridel, Jimenez, and Sapangdalaga in Misamis Occidental. His expertise in urban and rural planning will help these local governments optimize land resources for sustainable development. Iroy’s extensive experience in planning and environmental management equips him with the necessary skills to guide municipalities in crafting land use plans that balance development with environmental preservation. His work ensures that each area’s growth aligns with its unique needs, fostering long-term prosperity while maintaining the integrity of natural resources. Through his guidance, these municipalities will be better prepared to face urbanization challenges and promote sustainable living.
